1. Moolank (Root Number) – Your Core Personality

Moolank is derived by summing the digits of your birth day until a single digit (1–9). For example, if you were born on the 17th, 1+7 = 8, making your Moolank 8, ruled by Saturn (Shani). Moolank represents your innate nature, temperament, and the planetary energy that governs your soul. In Vedic texts, the Moolank is considered the “seed” of your karmic journey. It never changes. Our Free Vedic Numerology Calculator instantly reveals your root number and its ruling planet—Sun (1), Moon (2), Jupiter (3), Rahu (4), Mercury (5), Venus (6), Ketu (7), Saturn (8), or Mars (9).

2. Bhagyank (Destiny Number) – Your Life Path & Karma

Bhagyank is calculated by adding all digits of your full date of birth (DD+MM+YYYY) and reducing to a single digit. Example: 17-09-1994 → 1+7+0+9+1+9+9+4 = 40 → 4+0 = 4 (Rahu). Bhagyank reveals your life’s purpose, the opportunities and challenges you will face, and your ultimate destiny. While Moolank is the "why," Bhagyank is the "where you're going." The combination of Moolank and Bhagyank creates a unique numerological fingerprint. Use our calculator above to get both numbers instantly and explore the Vedic grid placement.

3. The Sacred 3×3 Vedic Grid (Numerology Chakra)

The Vedic Grid is a powerful 3×3 matrix where each cell holds a fixed number: top row (3,1,9) representing divine consciousness, middle row (6,7,5) representing earthly existence, and bottom row (2,8,4) representing the subconscious. Your birth digits, Moolank, and Bhagyank are placed onto this grid. Repeated digits indicate that a number’s planetary energy is amplified. For instance, three 8s in the grid indicate a strong Saturn influence—discipline, delays, and mastery. This tool visualizes your unique chakra, helping you identify missing numbers (challenges) and over-active numbers (gifts).

4. Mahadasha – The 100-Year Planetary Periods

Mahadasha is a uniquely Vedic dasha system where each planet rules for a specific number of years based on its value: Sun (1 yr), Moon (2 yrs), Jupiter (3 yrs), Rahu (4 yrs), Mercury (5 yrs), Venus (6 yrs), Ketu (7 yrs), Saturn (8 yrs), Mars (9 yrs). The sequence begins from your Moolank and then cycles through the numbers in ascending order (wrapping around). Our calculator generates your entire life's planetary periods, marking the start and end dates for each Mahadasha. Knowing your Mahadasha helps align major life transitions—career shifts, marriage, spiritual growth—with planetary rulers.
